Michigan Stadium Overview

Michigan Stadium, or as most people know it, "The Big House" is home to the Michigan Wolverines football team. It is famous for being the biggest stadium in the United States with a seated capacity of 107,601. Michigan Stadium is special because of its successful football team (most wins in college football history), its M GO BLUE Banner, its famous fight song "The Victors," and the winged helmets the football team wears. The stadium was build over 80 years ago, and has hosted numerous thrilling moments, so please see below a few of my favorites!
